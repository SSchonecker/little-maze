Messages to player:
	"Ouch! You lost " + pain + " hp..." : when player loses health
	"Oops! You lost your life..." : when players health reaches 0;
	"This is a spikey tile!" : when stepping on unrevealed spike tile
	"You move carefully past the spikes." : when stepping on revealed spike tile
	"Careful now! This looks like a spikey tile..." : when selecting a spike
	"You found the chest!" : when stepping on chest tile
	"You can't move there." : when position and orientation remain unchanged
	"There is a chest here!" : when selecting chest tile
	"This looks like an ordinary tile." : when selecting tile
	"You can't see this tile from here." : when not selecting neighbouring tile

	"Not a valid key." : exception message thrown by grid.
